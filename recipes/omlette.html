omlette

Description :

a dish made from eggs that are mixed together, cooked without stirring, and served folded in half often with a filling of cheese, vegetables, or meat.

Ingredients :

  • eggs
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • (as per wish can add some veggies !)

Making :

  1. take a pan and add 1-2 spoons of oil and let it warm up .
  2. the break the eggs and put them in a bowl and add salt and pepper and mix well
  3. now pour the mix into the pan and let it cook for a min and then reverse the omlette and let the other side cook for a min
